This customised system of yoga[6] combines postural yoga with chanting,
breathing (prāṇāyāma)[6] and meditation.

Kovikuttralam

Kovai Kutralam is a scenic spot with a gentle waterfall originating on
the Siruvani hill ranges. It is located on the western ghat mountain range
that lies to the west of this city at a distance of about 32 kms from Coimbatore.
The siruvani dam is just above this water fall and this place is under the control of state forest department.



Attapadi

History. Mannarkkad and Attappadi were parts of Valluvanad Swaroopam
